Introduction to our project.

Our research focuses on the use of Deep Learning models to generate new music. We plan to build an LSTM (1) network model implemented through Keras to input tokenized MIDI-3(2) files and predict notes according to the time-series (3) data. Our implementation applies extensive Music Theory concepts which are crucial to generate true-to-life music
